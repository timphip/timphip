### 🔬 Who Am I?
Welcome to my GitHub! I am a researcher and developer with a deep passion for underlying machine learning architectures. Instead of just using out-of-the-box models, I enjoy looking under the hood—optimizing post-training algorithms and introducing physical methods into neural networks to make them more robust and efficient. 

When I'm not writing code, you'll likely find me diving into academic papers on World Models or contributing to open-source convex optimization solvers like [CVXPY](https://github.com/cvxpy/cvxpy).

<br/>

### 🔭 Currently Exploring & Building
- 🌌 **Multimodal Learning & World Models:** Investigating geometric consistency using Optical Flow and Homography Estimation.
- ⚛️ **Physical Deep Learning:** Integrating traditional physical methods into modern Transformer architectures.
- ⏱️ **Low-Latency AI:** Building real-time, lightweight signal processing pipelines (e.g., EEG-based Brain-Computer Interfaces).
- 🧠 **Stanford CS Curriculum:** Actively upskilling through **CS231N** (CV), **CS231A** (3D Vision), and **CS224N** (NLP).
