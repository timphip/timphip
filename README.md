# Hi there, I'm Tim Zheng 👋

> 🧠 **AI Researcher & Software Developer**

> *"Exploring the intersection of deep learning, physical systems, and convex optimization."*

Welcome to my GitHub! I am a researcher and developer with a deep passion for underlying machine learning architectures and mathematical optimization. Instead of just using out-of-the-box models, I enjoy looking under the hood—optimizing post-training algorithms and introducing physical methods into neural networks to make them more robust and efficient.

When I'm not writing code, you'll likely find me diving into academic papers on World Models or contributing to open-source convex optimization solvers like [CVXPY](https://github.com/cvxpy/cvxpy).

### 🔭 What I'm currently working on & interested in:
- **Multimodal Learning & World Models:** Investigating geometric consistency in multimodal systems using Optical Flow and Homography Estimation.
- **Physical Deep Learning:** Exploring how traditional physical methods can be integrated into modern Transformer architectures.
- **Low-Latency AI Systems:** Building lightweight, real-time signal processing pipelines (such as EEG-based Brain-Computer Interfaces) under strict time constraints.
